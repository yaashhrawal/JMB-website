Our Story

Six decades of crafting authentic flavors and sweet memories

Welcome to JMB Udaipur

Since the 1960s, JMB Udaipur has been synonymous with authentic Rajasthani sweets and namkeen, bringing the royal flavors of Udaipur to homes across India.

Our journey began in the heart of the City of Lakes, where our founder started with a simple vision - to share the authentic taste of traditional Indian sweets with the world. What started as a small sweet shop has now grown into a beloved brand, trusted by thousands of families.

At JMB Udaipur, we believe in preserving the age-old recipes while embracing modern quality standards. Every product is crafted with the same love and dedication that our founder instilled six decades ago.
