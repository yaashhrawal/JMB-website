Our Sweet Legacy

Celebrating 60+ years of authentic Rajasthani flavors and traditional craftsmanship

The Beginning of a Sweet Journey

In the 1960s, in the heart of Udaipur's royal city, JMB was born from a simple dream - to share the authentic taste of Rajasthani sweets with the world. What started as a small family business has grown into a legacy of trust, quality, and uncompromising excellence.

Our founders believed that every sweet should tell a story, carry a tradition, and bring families together. This philosophy continues to guide us today as we craft each product with the same passion and attention to detail.

Our Journey Through Time

Six decades of sweet milestones and cherished memories

1960s

The Foundation

JMB Udaipur was established with a commitment to authentic Rajasthani sweets and traditional recipes passed down through generations.

1980s

Expansion & Recognition

Expanded our product range and gained recognition across Rajasthan for maintaining authentic taste and premium quality.

2000s

Modern Innovation

Introduced modern packaging and preservation techniques while maintaining traditional recipes and taste.

2020s

Digital Transformation

Launched online platform to serve customers nationwide, bringing authentic Udaipur sweets to every corner of India.
